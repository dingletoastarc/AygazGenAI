About Us

At HowDoesThatFeel, we believe in the transformative power of empathy. Our mission is to revolutionize the way individuals perceive and understand the diverse spectrum of human emotions, paving the way for a more empathetic world. Rooted in the profound insights of psychology and propelled by a relentless dedication to societal betterment, we endeavor to foster connections, bridge divides, and inspire profound moments of understanding.

Led by visionary founder Ayberk Ozen, a distinguished scholar and advocate for mental wellness, HowDoesThatFeel stands as a beacon of compassion in a world often marked by indifference. With a deep-seated commitment to unraveling the complexities of human emotions, Ayberk's pioneering work has garnered international acclaim, earning him recognition as a trailblazer in the field of empathy research.

Drawing upon cutting-edge psychological principles and innovative technologies, our platform offers a unique opportunity for individuals to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of human experiences. Through interactive experiences, thought-provoking content, and immersive simulations, users are invited to step into the shoes of others, gaining invaluable insights into the myriad emotions that shape our collective human experience.

At HowDoesThatFeel, we envision a future where empathy transcends boundaries, uniting us in our shared humanity and propelling us toward a more compassionate and inclusive world. Join us on this journey of discovery, understanding, and empowerment as we strive to make empathy not just a concept, but a way of life.
